About CampusNotify

CampusNotify is a centralized platform developed by students of Aditya Engineering College to keep the student community informed about all campus-related opportunities like hackathons, internships, college events, and club activities.

Our Mission

To bridge the communication gap between students and organizers by offering a transparent and accessible notification system for every campus activity and opportunity.

Our College

We proudly represent Aditya Engineering College, a hub of innovation and talent. Our platform reflects the vibrant campus culture and aims to empower every student with timely information.

Campus Clubs

CampusNotify keeps you informed about the latest updates on internships, hackathons, technical and cultural events, and sports. It also covers Rotaract Club, NSS, NCC, and the Red Cross Society.

Technologies Used

  • HTML - for structuring web content
  • CSS – for styling and layout
  • JavaScript – for interactivity and smooth animations

Vision for the Future

We aim to integrate login functionality, organizer dashboards, event registration analytics, and mobile responsiveness in future versions of CampusNotify to serve students better and smarter.
